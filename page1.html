What will you experience?

Marco will show you its winery and will tell you the secrets of his land and his wines: here you will be able to discover the amazing job of an authentic winemaker and know how Langhe e Roero wines are produced.

After the visit, Marco will offer you a wine tasting in his recent-restored hall located in the old winery, served with some typical local snacks. You will be able to taste:

  • 4 wines: Roero Arneis - Langhe Nebbiolo - Barbera d'Alba "Mommiano" - Roero "Torretta" (15 € per person) 
  • 6 wines: Roero Arneis - Roero Arneis "Camestri" - Langhe Nebbiolo - Barbera d'Alba "Mommiano" - Roero "Torretta" - Roero "Torretta" vintage (24 € per person)

The visit lasts around 1 hour, included the wine tasting session. At the end of the visit you will have the opportunity to buy Marco's wine directly from him at a competitive price and with the advice of an expert winemaker and his collaborators.

About "Porello Vini"

Marco Porello is an authentic winemaker from Roero's area. His family run the company since 1930, when his grandfather, Cesare Porello, transformed their small property in a proper establishment dedicated to the production of wine. 

Today Marco run the farmhouse with a great attenti
on to quality and respect for tradition, without fearing to experiment new concept and new ideas. 

Other info

Childrens are welcomed. The visit is free for people under 18 but they won't be allowed to taste wine. Childrens have to be accompanied by an adult during the whole visit and cannot be left alone during the visit and the wine tasting.
