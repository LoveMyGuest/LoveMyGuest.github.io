What will you experience?

Marco will bring you in the heart of his vineyards of Barbera and Nebbiolo, located in the hills of Canale and Vezza d'Alba. Here you will be able to discover beauties of his land, with the correct density and the right tenor of clay necessary for the perfect ripeness of grapes. 

Once arrived at his Ciabot (a typical piedmont's building located in the heart of vineyards and used as storage), you will be able to enjoy the wonderful panorama on the hills and taste his wines served with some typical snacks. 

During this experience you will be able to taste: 

  • 2 wines: Roero Arneis "Camestri" - Roero "Torretta" (50 € per person - wine tasting hold in the ciabot or in the winery, accordingly to availability) 
  • 6 wines: Roero Arneis - Roero Arneis "Camestri" - Langhe Nebbiolo - Barbera d'Alba "Mommiano" - Roero "Torretta" - Roero "Torretta" vintage (65 € per person - in this case wines tasting will take place entirely in the Porello vini winery)

The whole visit lasts around 3 hours, included the wine tasting session. At the end of the visit you will have the opportunity to buy Marco's wine directly from him at a competitive price and with the advice of an expert winemaker and his collaborators.

About "Porello Vini"

Marco Porello is an authentic winemaker from Roero's area. His family run the company since 1930, when his grandfather, Cesare Porello, transformed their small property in a proper establishment dedicated to the production of wine. 

Today Marco run the farmhouse with a great attenti on to quality and respect for tradition, without fearing to experiment new concept and new ideas. 

Other info

Childrens are welcomed. The visit is free for people under 18 but they won't be allowed to taste wine. Childrens have to be accompanied by an adult during the whole visit and cannot be left alone during the visit and the wine tasting.
Comfortable clothes and shoes are suggested, considering the walk will be in on the hills.
