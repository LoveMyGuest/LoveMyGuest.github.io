What will you experience?

For all bike enthusiasts, but also for simple nature lovers looking for new adventures. This exciting half-day trip will take you around the "crus" of Barolo, biking for around 35 km through some of the most evocative landscapes of the Langhe. Together with you for guaranteeing your safety there will be qualified guides who will show you, step-by-step, the different treasures of a land still to be discovered (from the Castle of Grinzane to the great Cedar of Lebanon, passing by the suggestive Big Banch and the most famous villages of that area). 
 
Thanks to the expert local guide of the territory you will not waste time reading maps and you will be able to enjoy the ride and the glamorous sightseeing without stressing out!
 
The excursion, which take place mainly off road between passes in the vineyard and paths, is suitable for everyone, including children, thanks to the use of electric mountain-bikes that will allow you to focus on the gems of this territory without any worries and excessive physical effort
 
From time to time new corners of the territory will appear to you, while, the tours may vary according to suitable paths of the day and also according to the guests preferences, their level and time available.

The tour details are:

  • Barolo Crus Tour: 35 km-long tour in the "Crus" of Barolo, departing from the Alba city center, passing through, Diano D'Alba, Grinzane Cavour and La Morra, Roddi and coming back to Alba. (60 € per person - + 10 € one-time if other departure from Alba city center for the delivery of the mountain-bike)

The whole excursion lasts about half a day and the price includes a qualified guide, electric mountain-bike rental, helmet and water bottle.

About Nicola & Paolo

Nicola and Paolo are two brothers passionate about MTB bikes, lovers of our land, nature and our traditions.

They strongly trust that Langhe and Roero are territories that deserve to be visited in a deep and natural way, on foot or by bike. 

Their passion for MTB (both Paolo and Nicola are MTB certified guides) and the love for their land, brought them to start this activity, for promoting the mountain-bike sport and this new way to travel.. 

Other info

Everyone and guests of all ages are welcome, as depending on the skills, the guide can vary the route.

The tour is free for children up to 12 years but the bike is not included in the price: to bring your children with you, we will provide you with a child bike seat, (max 25 kg). In case you would like a dedicated bike for your children,we will manage that specifically under request and according to availability (unfortunately, the availability of electric mountain bikes for children under 12 is reduced, but we will do our best to get it).

Electric mountain bikes can accommodate each type of tourist, but taking into account that it is a sports excursion, a proper mountain biking technique is required
 
It requires sportswear and sneakers. 
In case of bad weather the tour could be canceled, with a corresponding 100% refund
