La Moscateria "Ai Ciuvin"

Vineyard tour, Moscato tasting and culinary experience 
at Ai Ciuvin

Live a unique experience strolling through the striking hillsides of the Sorì vineyards, discover the Moscato's secrets in the Moscateria cellar-museum and enjoy local Piedmontese dishes

The only occasion for enjoying such a complete experience, entirely dedicated to Moscato d'Asti, the wine of Cesare Pavese and beppe Fenoglio's land.

What will you experience?

xxxx 

  • 6 wines: Barbaresco "Colle Gelso" - Langhe Arneis "Le Rune" - Langhe Nebbiolo "La Riddolina" - Barbera d'Asti "Convento" & "San Lorenzo" - Dolcetto d'Alba "Corte delle viole" -  (22 € per person)
The visit lasts around 2 hours, included the wine tasting session. At the end of the visit you will have the opportunity to buy Maura's wines directly from her at a competitive price and benefitting from the advice of an expert winemaker and her collaborators.

Why your host suggests you this visit?

As soon as you enter in Cascina Sarìa winery you feel like beeing at home. The atmosphere is the typical one of a family-run winery, where wine is their life and wineyards are its treasures.

The warm welcome of Maura, as well as the more tymid but meaningful welcome of his father will immediate let you understand that you found a pearl of Langhe region.

Childrens are welcomed. The visit is free for people under 18 but they won't be allowed to taste wine. Childrens have to be accompanied by an adult during the whole visit and cannot be left alone during the visit and the wine tasting.
