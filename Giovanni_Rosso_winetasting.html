What will you experience?

Reaching Giovanni Rosso winery from Alba, you will have the opportunity to pass through the village of Serralunga d'Alba (Seralonga d'Alba in Piedmontese) and overtake is magnificent castle, one of the best-preserved examples of noble castle fourteenth-century of Piedmont.

Just after leaving behind the castle of serralunga, you will reach the Giovanni Rosso winery: immediately you will perceive the care for details and the importance of the project carried out by Davide Rosso, Giovanni's son.

Francesca will be there for welcoming you and narrating you the important story of Vigna Rionda and the ancient origin of this leading winery. Then, she will lead you through the vinification area and the ageing room: here, you will get an interesting description of every production process and you will have the opportunity to ask your questions directly to Davide or his staff.

The winetasting will take place directly in the ageing room, where you will taste some Barolo Cru straight from the barrel: there is no better way to truly understand wine while creating such memorable moments.

In particular, you will be taste:

  • 3 Wines from the barrel: Barolo d.o.c,g "del Comune di Serralunga d'Alba", Barolo d.o.c,g "Cerretta" and Barolo d.o.c,g "Serra"
  • 3 Wines From bottle: Langhe d.o.c. Nebbiolo, Barolo d.o.c.g. and Etna Bianco d.o.p

The visit lasts around 1 hour and at the end of the visit you will have the opportunity to buy Giovanni Rosso's wines choosing among a great variety of top-quality red wines.

About "Giovanni Rosso"

Azienda Agricola Giovanni Rosso is a small, family-owned producer in the heart of the Barolo commune of Serralunga d'Alba. They only produce red wines and are today one of the most accomplished winemaker of the region.

Since the 1890's the Rosso family has farmed vineyards in Serralunga d'Alba, notably the Crus of Cerretta, La Serra, Broglio, Meriame, Sorano, Costa Bella, Lirano & Damiano. During the early 1980's Giovanni Rosso restructured the vineyards with the aim of growing the best grapes.

Today, the winery is run by Davide, Giovanni's son. Thanks to his oenology studies and his experience abroad, notably in France, he took charge of the vinification & aging of the wines with one sentence in mind: "wine should be a perfect copy of its Terroir". 

Davide is unstoppable and with his trusted staff, he churns out project after project and is contributing to place Barolo and his vineyards in a leading role in the world stage of wine. 

The Giovanni Rosso winery is located in the hamlet of Baudana, just a couple of kilometers north of Serralunga d'Alba village: do not miss the opportunity to visit its magnificent castle, one of the best-preserved examples of noble castle fourteenth-century of Piedmont.
