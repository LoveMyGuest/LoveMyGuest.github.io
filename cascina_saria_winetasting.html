What will you experience?

Maura will show you its winery and will tell you the secrets of his land and his wines: here you will be able to discover the amazing job of an authentic winemaker and know how Langhe wines are produced.

After the visit, Maura will offer you a wine tasting in his recent-restored hall located in the old winery, served with some typical local snacks. You will be able to taste:

  • 6 wines: Langhe Arneis "Le Rune" - Langhe Nebbiolo "La Riddolina"- Barbera d'Asti "Convento" - Barbera d'Asti "San Lorenzo" -  Dolcetto d'Alba "Corte delle Viole" - Barbaresco "Colle Gelso" (25 € per person )

The visit lasts around 1 hour, included the wine tasting session. At the end of the visit you will have the opportunity to buy Maura's wines directly from her at a competitive price and with the advice of an expert winemaker and her collaborators.

About "Porello Vini"

The winery was founded shortly after 1970 with the purchase of an old farmhouse to be restored and modest land to be worked.

In 1999 Cascina Sarìa started the production of bottled wines ready to meet the requirements of the most demanding customer. After many years of experience in close contact with our land and every single type of vine, we were able to start that difficult, slow and delicate process of transforming grapes into wine.

Our production is exclusively from grapes of our own vineyards and is oriented towards low yields per hectare, so the amount is limited but guaranteed.

Cascina Sarìa is located between Langhe and Monferrato, in Neive, an enchanting old village of medieval origin.
