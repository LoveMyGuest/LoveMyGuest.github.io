What will you experience?

Maura will welcome you at Cascina Saria with her warm smile, introducing you in her winery located just few minute from the wonderful medieval village of Neive.

Weather permitting, you will start the tour strolling through their Barbaresco and Chardonnay vineyards, entirely surrounding the winery. Maura will tell you about his father technique for managing the vines and about the commitment and the attentions necessaries for producing high quality wines, paying attention to market demands but without ever distorting your product.

During the visit, you will be able to gain fascinating insights into the production processes on a tour of the winery before quenching your thirst by sampling a selection of Cascina Saria's wines with the expert guidance of Maura: the wonderful view on the hills and Alps you will be able to enjoy from the winetasting room will leave you dumbfounded.  

Of course, according to typical Piedmont's tradition, the tasting session will be served with some typical local snacks like cheeses and cured meats, coming from Cascina Saria's partners (and friends).

The tasting session will include:

  • 4 wines: Langhe Arneis "Le Rune" -  Barbera d'Asti "Convento" - Dolcetto d'Alba "Corte delle Viole" - Barbaresco "Colle del Gelso" (15 € per person )
  • 7 wines: Langhe Arneis "Le Rune" - Spumante Brut "Sarì" - Langhe Nebbiolo "La Riddolina"- Barbera d'Asti "Convento" - Barbera d'Asti "San Lorenzo" -  Dolcetto d'Alba "Corte delle Viole" - Barbaresco "Colle del Gelso" (25 € per person )

The visit lasts around 2 hours, included the wine tasting session. At the end of the visit you will have the opportunity to buy Cascina Saria wines directly from Maura at a competitive price and with the advice of an expert winemaker and her collaborators.

About "Cascina Sarìa"

It is right on the doorstep of one of Italy's loveliest villages, Neive, that you can find the small family run estate of Cascina Saria.

The winery was founded shortly after 1970 when Maura's father bought an old farmhouse to be restored and a modest land to be worked. After many years of experience in close contact with their land and every single type of vine, he started the difficult, slow and delicate process of transforming grapes into wine.  

Today, Cascina Saria it is run with a fierce determination to excel by a strong and a close team made by Maura's family.

Their passion for their job, their low-yields production technique and their wonderful location in the heart of thlee Langhe, a terroir that is some of the best in Italy, makes success almost guaranteed.  Actually, the estate falls partly in the traditional Barbaresco producing region of Neive and partly into that of Coazzolo and Castagnole, renowned for Barbera d'Asti. The result is, not surprisingly, excellent wines.
