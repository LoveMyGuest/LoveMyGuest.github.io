What will you experience?

We are in the the lands narrated by Fenoglio & Pavese, a place where rugged path and rolling hills follow one another. Here, on the right of the Tanaro river, you will discover the charm of the first "Moscateria" of Italy, where all wines arise from a typical grapes of Pidmont: the Moscato grapes. 

You will experience a suggestive tour starting from the place where everithing is origined, a typical and very steep wineyard called "Sorì".  Here Simone will tell you the story of the land and he will explain you his farming technique. After that, he will lead you through the old cellar that today hosts the little farming museum of La Moscateria.

You will continue the experience with the  the two Moscato wines produced  with the wine tasting, served with some typical snacks.

Finally, if you desire, you will be able to finish your tour with "ad-hoc" studied lunch prepared by Simone himself.

Hence, you will be able to choose between: 

  • Moscateria tour with Moscato wine tasting: Sorì, Cellar and La Moscateria visit with tasting of 2 Moscato wines and Grappa di Moscato (18€ per adult)
  • Moscateria tour with Moscato wine tasting and lunch: as above, with included a special lunch with dishes prepared by Simone himself (36€ per adult - wines excluded)

Before and during the lunch, you will be able to taste other wines produced by Simone at Agriturismo Ai Ciuvin as Barbera d'asti and Langhe Arneis. 

The visit lasts around 1,5 - 2 hours, excluded the lunch. At the end of the visit you will have the opportunity to buy Simone's wines chosing among Moscato, Barbera d'Asti, Langhe Arneis and Grappa di Moscato.

About "La Moscateria Ai Ciuvin"

La Moscateria is a real 360* tour around Moscato grapes, a benchmark for those who desire to know this special grape and this unique place proposing this wine in all his kind of vinification.

The Agriturismo of Simone Cerutti and family is one of the first born in Piedmont in the 80's and it is managed everyday as it would be the first day and with the same passion. A real guarantee of quality!

The little farming museum obtained in the old cellar of 1900, is a real jewel: here you could discover the original farming tools used at the time as the old sack filter, the pressing machine and the concrete tank.
Moreover, you could visit the old "Crutin", an ancient room manually escavated in the rock for stocking and conserving the wines after its bottling.
